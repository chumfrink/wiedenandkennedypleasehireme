I was born and raised in the San Fernando Valley, where I was exposed to production from a young age. I poured over VHS tapes at my local video store, reading the covers front to back. I can still recall vividly watching my first behind-the-scenes feature on the Lord of the Rings Special Edition DVD set. I had an inkling then that there was something calling me to this work.

At the age of 17, with no reasonable forethought, I took a train and two buses to Hollywood, driven by a desire to learn the craft. After spotting a camera crew, I asked if I could help out. The producer gave me $50 to buy gum and water, which I did. When I returned, he praised me for not stealing his money. He then tasked me with my first ever "lock-up", where I was promptly physically assaulted by a rum-drunk Jack Sparrow impersonator.

Needless to say...I was elated. In heaven. On cloud nine.

Let's dive into some of what I've done, and what I can do.

What I've Done & What I Can Do
